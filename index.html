About DAYEHS

Distinct Advocacy For Youth Empowerment And Healthier Society (DAYEHS) is a registered and certified non-governmental organization (NGO) by the Federal Government of Nigeria (RN7133392). We are dedicated to fostering positive change within our society, operating as a non-religious and non-political entity.

WHY DAYEHS?

The escalating levels of social vices, insecurity, and moral decadence among today's youth are deeply concerning. These trends can be traced back to various factors including widespread joblessness, limited access to education, lack of clear vision, and inadequate orientation. If left unchecked, this trajectory threatens to undermine the future of generations to come. DAYEHS stands as a beacon of hope amidst these challenges, committed to redirecting the focus of young minds towards key areas of nation-building. Our mission encompasses instilling the right values, fostering innovative creativity, and promoting constructive productivity among the youth.

OUR BELIEFS

POSSIBILITY:

At DAYEHS, we firmly believe in the attainability of a better and healthier society. This vision hinges on the genuine determination and positive actions of both leaders and citizens. Leaders must exemplify positivity, objectivity, and accountability, while citizens must uphold the rule of law and be willing to make sacrifices for the collective good.



COLLECTIVE RESPONSIBILITY:

We advocate for a paradigm shift towards a culture of collective responsibility. Every individual, institution, agency, and organization has a crucial role to play in shaping a better society. DAYEHS is deeply passionate about promoting this ethos of collective action, viewing it as our contribution to securing a brighter future for generations to come.



PARADIGM SHIFT:

achieve lasting change, there must be a fundamental shift in our societal values. We cannot expect different outcomes by perpetuating the same patterns of behavior. DAYEHS advocates for a paradigm shift towards values that prioritize integrity, empathy, and social responsibility.




IDENTIFYING THE OBSTACLE:

Corruption emerges as a formidable obstacle to societal development. At DAYEHS, we staunchly oppose corruption and advocate for a zero-tolerance approach to eradicate this scourge.



VANITY:

DAYEHS acknowledges the harsh reality of mortality, using it as a powerful tool to deter corrupt practices and unethical behavior. We advocate for responsible stewardship of resources and a focus on leaving a positive legacy rather than pursuing transient gains.

DAYEHS Mission and Objectives


At DAYEHS, our mission is to empower young people to realize their full potential and become catalysts for positive change in their communities. Through a series of dynamic initiatives, we aim to address pressing social issues and foster a culture of innovation, integrity, and compassion. Our objectives are as follows:

1. Empowerment Through Education: We strive to organize free sensitization and enlightenment programs for young people, equipping them with the necessary skills and knowledge to thrive in entrepreneurial ventures. By providing practical training and mentorship, we empower individuals to take control of their futures and succeed in the competitive business landscape.



2. Inspiring Excellence:

Our goal is to inspire youth to aspire for greatness and become the best versions of themselves. Through motivational workshops and mentorship programs, we encourage young people to embrace entrepreneurship as a pathway to personal and professional growth. By instilling a mindset of resilience and ambition, we aim to cultivate a generation of innovative leaders and change-makers.



3. Promoting Ethics and Values:

We are committed to launching aggressive campaigns to promote moral values and ethical standards within our society. By raising awareness and fostering dialogue, we seek to combat social vices and uphold integrity in all aspects of life. Through advocacy and community engagement, we strive to create a culture of accountability and respect for one another.



4. Fostering Entrepreneurship:

DAYEHS aims to inspire young people to shift their mindset from being job seekers to becoming job creators. Through entrepreneurship training and support programs, we empower individuals to identify opportunities, develop innovative solutions, and launch sustainable businesses. By fostering an entrepreneurial ecosystem, we aim to drive economic growth and create pathways to prosperity for all.



5. Addressing Social Challenges:

We are dedicated to contributing to the government's efforts in reducing poverty, unemployment, and insecurity. Through targeted interventions and collaborative partnerships, we seek to address the root causes of these issues and create opportunities for sustainable development. By empowering marginalized communities and promoting inclusive policies, we aim to build a more equitable and prosperous society for future generations.



6. Enhancing Environmental Sustainability:

DAYEHS is committed to improving the quality of life by promoting a healthier environment. Through awareness campaigns and community projects, we advocate for sustainable practices and conservation efforts. By fostering environmental stewardship and resilience, we aim to safeguard natural resources and create a greener, more sustainable future for all.



7. Providing Humanitarian Assistance:

We recognize the importance of extending a helping hand to those in need. DAYEHS is dedicated to providing humanitarian relief to less privileged individuals and communities facing hardship. Through outreach programs and support initiatives, we aim to alleviate suffering, restore hope, and create opportunities for a brighter tomorrow.

At DAYEHS, we believe that by empowering young people and fostering a culture of collaboration and innovation, we can create positive change and build a brighter future for all. Join us in our mission to transform lives and make a difference in the world. Together, we can achieve greatness and leave a lasting legacy of impact and inspiration.
