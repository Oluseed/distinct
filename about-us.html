WHO WE ARE

DAYEHS is a registered and certified Non-Governmental Organization, dedicated to fostering positive change within our society, operating as a non-religious and non-political entity.

MISSION:

Our mission is to empower young people to realize their full potentials and become catalysts for positive change. Through fostering a culture of innovation, integrity, and compassion, we aim to address pressing social issues and create a better and safer society for all

VISION
Our vision is to make a profound and lasting impact on society, becoming a global point of reference in humanitarian relief efforts, entrepreneurship education, and community development.

Discover more

Core Values

Honesty: We uphold the highest standards of honesty and transparency in all our endeavors, fostering trust and accountability within our organization and beyond.

Integrity:: We are committed to conducting ourselves with integrity, adhering to ethical principles and moral values in every decision and action we take.

Service: We believe in the power of service to humanity, dedicating ourselves to serving others selflessly and making a positive difference in the lives of those in need.

Motto: "Service To Humanity" - This motto encapsulates our unwavering commitment to serving others with compassion, empathy, and dedication, making the world a better place for all.
